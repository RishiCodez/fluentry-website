Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Fluentry?

Simply download the app from the App Store, create an account, and take our quick assessment. Our AI will determine your current English level and create a personalized learning path just for you.

Is Fluentry suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Fluentry is designed for learners at all levels, from complete beginners to advanced users. Our AI adapts to your proficiency level and creates appropriate lessons for you.

How does the AI lesson generator work?

Our AI analyzes your interests, current level, and learning goals to create custom lessons on topics that matter to you. Simply tell us what you want to learn, and we'll generate engaging content instantly.

Can I practice speaking English in the app?

Yes! Fluentry includes conversation practice with AI tutors who provide instant feedback. You can also practice pronunciation with our speaking exercises.

How do the widgets work?

Long press on your home screen, tap the plus icon, search for Fluentry, and add your preferred widget size. You can customize what information is displayed in the widget settings.

What happens if I miss a day of my streak?

Your streak will reset to zero, but don't worry! The important thing is building consistent learning habits. Start a new streak and keep going.

Can I use Fluentry offline?

You can review your saved vocabulary offline. However, AI-powered features like lesson generation and conversation practice require an internet connection.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings in the app, scroll down to Account, and select Delete Account. Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We take your privacy seriously. All your data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal information with third parties. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

How often is new content added?

We regularly update the app with new features, vocabulary, and exercises. Plus, with our AI lesson generator, you have access to unlimited fresh content on any topic.
